PersLEARN

Introduction

This project provides a comprehensive solution for academic researchers and beginners in their academic learning and research processes, including literature search, reading, organization, sharing, literature classification management, and writing reviews, all from a perspective- and viewpoint-oriented approach. Furthermore, within the established ecosystem, users assume dual roles as both ‘producers’ and ‘consumers,’ enabling them to publish content for the purpose of promoting their viewpoints and work, while also acquiring content for research and learning.

Gragh system

In the graph system, users primarily read papers and extract key information from the articles as evidence, which they can then use to construct explanations. After forming nodes in the graph, relationships can be established between different nodes, ultimately resulting in shareable visualizations.

Design details

In terms of design style, we aim for knowledge to be the visual focal point,and follow the Light, Clean, Rugular style.

  • Lignt: Use bright colors to represent knowledge nodes.
  • Clean: Reduce interference from extraneous visual elements.
  • Rugular: Utilize a conventional left-right layout to reduce cognitive load.
